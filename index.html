One of my most favorite main dish is - Sweet Pongal

Sweet pongal is a traditional south indian dish made with ghee, jaggery, cardamoms and nuts. The dish is said to celebrate our ancestors prosperity after their harvest.

How to make cup of chai

  1. Add water to boil in a hot bowl
  2. Add chai leaves
    1. Use a Spoon to stir the boiling leaves.
    2. Let it sit for 5 minutes on the stove.
  3. Add sugar and milk according to your choice.
